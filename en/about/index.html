ABOUT

Daniil Menshov.

Frontend developer. Based in Gdańsk. I work alone.

Bio

Websites have been my main focus for the last year and a half: landings, multipage, small e-commerce. I run the project end-to-end — from the first conversation about the brief to deployment on a live domain. No agency overhead, no handoffs between specialists, no "that's not my zone".

How I work

A calm approach: first understand the niche and audience, then structure and copy, then design, then code. At every step I show the result and we agree — then move on. No surprises at the end.

What I don't take

  • Projects where someone brings finished design and wants it "just coded" without any discussion. Without initial analysis the result is worse than it could be.
  • E-commerce above 500 SKU — needs a different stack and a team.
  • Subcontracting for creative agencies — they sell a different thing, and it'll be a compromise for everyone.
  • Rush "needed yesterday" briefs. A good site needs time for analysis and sign-offs.
